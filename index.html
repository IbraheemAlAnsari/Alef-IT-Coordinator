Dear All,

Greeting from IT Coordinators,

The purpose of writing the following Email is to give exact guidelines on how to fill a change request for and described the role of ALef IT OST when filling Alef IT Change Request Form.

Below I have attached an editable PDF which will allow all OSTs to only edit the required field on the form and not edit the form itself.

1.  In the Request Details Section, below details are required to be filled correctly in order for the back office teams to resolve the issues as soon as possible:

a.  Name:  We need the name of the requester (Principal, Vice Principal, Social Worker)

b. Job Title: We need the job title of the requester who has requested the following change request.

c. Contact Details: We need the Contact number and the Email of the requester.

d. Schools Details: School Name & the School ID of the school where the change is requested from.

e. Description of Request: Briefly describe the change requested and what is the impact of the following request on the existing infrastructure.

f. Reason for change: Justify why the following change has been requested.

2. Alef Site Survey section has to be completed by OST and just leave the Cost & Delivery Time for Alef IT back office team.

a. Service Type: In the following feild please select one of the following:

Hardware: (For Example Charging Cart, Chormobook, Head Set)
Service (For Example: Shifting Access Point, Shifting WAP)
Both (For Example: Add new Access Point)

The following has to be marked for any request from the school (AP, CC, HD, CB)

b. Description: Please mention what is required and what is the existing issue and why is it required and what is available on site. (For example 1. AP is required in Class 6A - Class 6A already has Huwaie AP & it only required to be replaced. 2. AP is required in Class 6B - Class 6B does not have any access point, therefore, it required the cable to be pulled and new access point to be installed. 3. Charging Cart Required in 6D: 6D is a new class with 27 students and has a free power socket for the charging cart to power on.

c. Cost & Delivery Time: Please leave it for IT CC

d. Site Readiness Status: Give a brief explanation on the status of the classroom if the following class is ready for AP/CC to be installed, or to have the charging cart etc. For example, As requested classroom 6A needs AP and its ready as there is an empty space on the ceiling and there a place to pull the cable from.

3. Principal Approval: Just make sure the full name of the principal is mentioned along with all the fields completed and the school has stamped the following form.

Rest of the sections please leave them empty, I have attached a sample change request for your reference.

Dear Team,

For your action to follow with respect to project change request process. 

All CR forms to be submitted to MOE should be in this format. 

Here I would like to highlight few points noted from some ticket I reviewed. 

There is a requirement for improving clarity on CR submitted, IT onsite team representing Alef, need to understand the business requirements from school (principal) and convert it to IT requirements and update site survey part accordingly. 

For example, principal will say that there is a new class with 30 students in some area of school for Alef because of new students joined from other schools. 
The above mentioned is a business requirement. Now you have to convert it to IT requirements by gathering more information from school admin.
1) request for new floor plan with new room marked.
2) visit the room and check the requirements and feasibility for implementing IT infrastructure there. (Check if AP is available, is cabling required, is power sockets available for CC, is there a projector available, is there any other dependency etc.)
3) how many new students?
4) does the new class require CC etc.

Once we have all requirements, we need to submit just one CR form with all requirements gathered in the form.

Final form will have business requirement in the first part and IT requirements in the second part. (Hardware, service or both) . This form should be submitted in a ticket in SD with new floor plan (clear scan copy). It is unfortunate that some times I see scanned copy of floor plan not in proper way, which is very silly(upside down, not clear, photos, sides cut with partial information, floor plan without marking etc)

Please put your full dedication to improve above mentioned points.

All the best.

Best Regards
Sajin.

Dear All,

Please find attached the new incident report V3 which has to be filled for all the damaged asset incidents from 1st September 2019 even the ones which have been already filled and uploaded on the service desk (All old forms which have been created from 1st September 2019 till today have to be replaced with the new incident report V3)

Points to follow while filling the form:

1. Fill in the details of the asset owner(Student/Teacher) and please make sure all fields are filled in as shown below:

a. In the date section, make sure to add the exact date of the incident.

b. In the name row please make sure to fill in the first name and the last name.

c. The designation will be Student / Teacher (subject) / Principal / Vice Principal.

d. School Name will be your School Name as per the ESIS and make sure the spelling is correct.

e. In the staff/student ID row, please make sure the student or staff ID must be as Esis / Manhal / ERP No.

f. In the staff/student contact details, please make sure that if it is damaged by staff please enter the staff contact details and if it is damaged by a student please enter the student guardian contact details.

g. In grade/section/staff department please mention as follows for students: 6A (sample data) and for teachers: Maths Teacher Grade 8 (sample data)

h. In school ID section please enter the exact school ID as per Esis/Manhal system.

2. Fill in the Damaged IT Equipment section where all asset details are clearly mentioned along with the Alef Service Desk Incident number and also describe clearly how the incident occurred and who is responsible for causing the asset damage and what has been damaged in this incident.

a. Asset no. should be filled with the asset tag found under the ChromeBook / Lenovo Laptop - In case it's not found please check the IT CC sheet which will be shared by mid of October.

b. Alef SD Incident ID no will be the incident ticket number from Alef Service desk for Chromebooks and MOE Service Desk for Windows Laptop.

c. In the description of the incident field, describes how the incident occurred and who is responsible for causing the asset damage and what has been damaged in this incident.

d. The cost of device repair will be evaluated by the OST (a reference to the price sheet) which is attached on the following email and to be shared to all OST's by the management and the evaluated price to be mentioned in the total repair cost part.

3. In the principal declaration section, please make sure that all field should be filled and to be validated by OST.

4.  Below is the case by case resolution based on the principle declaration selection:
 
a. In case when the principal selects Student Guardian options as responsible for bearing the asset cost, Alef OSTs should not replace the asset until the guardian arrives at the school and fills the guardian declaration form***

b. In case when the principal selects Staff or School/MOE/ADEK options as responsible for bearing the asset repair cost, Alef OSTs should not replace the asset until the form is completed and stamped by the school management ***

c. In case when the principal selects Manufacturer Defect & Alef OSTs should validate the following device is manufacture defect and it's not working from the starting ***

5. In the Alef Education Internal Use section, the OST must mention what action has been taken and mention the replacement serial number in case the asset has been replaced.

A sample Incident form has been attached for your reference to see how we expect the incident form to be, any incomplete incident form will not be accepted by Alef Education.

Please find below the process for a Student / Teacher / Admin leaving the school or shifting the department:

1. Students / Teacher / Admin Leaving the School

a. The asset allocated to the student (Acer Chromebook / Lenovo / Headsets / Mice) has to be collected and inspected to see if there is no damage to the device by the OST.

a. The asset allocated to the student (Acer Chromebook / Lenovo / Headsets / Mice) has to be collected and shifted to the new class by the OST.
b. Once the assets have been shifted to the new class the OST must raise a "User Moving" ticket on the service desk for them to update the student department and to complete other SD / OST related tasks.
